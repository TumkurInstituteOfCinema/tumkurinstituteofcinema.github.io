About Institution

We are Creative Tech Enthusiast working since 2020

MK Shankar - MK Shankar did Post graduation in Cinema and thereafter started working as freelance film maker. He has produced and directed more than seventy five Documentaries, three Hindi feature films and various corporate films and commercials (Pidilite, Thermax, Crompton, L&T to name a few). His award winning films include Solution (1986), Silent Revolution (1991) The resurgence (2003) and the Messengers (2004). He is a visiting faculty at various film institutes in Mumbai and Maharashtra and also a selection committee member for MIFF (2010). Shubhra Datta - did her Post graduation in Mass communication from Jadhavpur University and thereafter joined SRFTI, Kolkata to pursue her studies in Film making. As a student of Cinematography she excelled in lighting techniques. Under the able guidance of internationally acclaimed Cinematographer Subrata Mitra (Pather Panchali, Aparajito, Apur Sansar) Shubra Datta learnt the finer nuances of lighting techniques and cinematography for three years at SRFTI. Since 1997, she worked as a freelance film maker simultaneously assisting some of the top – notch Cinematographers in the film industry. The projects include Munna Bhai MBBS, Shikhar, Delhi – 6, Mausam to name a few. Her films ‘The Seed keepers and ‘Ladies Special’ won the prestigious national awards.

Shailendra Singh ‘Shellee’ - Shailendra Singh Sodhi commonly known as ‘Shellee’ is a poet, film lyricist and writer. He has worked as lyricist and background music composer for various Hindi films including the critically acclaimed Dev D, Udta Punjab and other films like Luv shuvte chicken khurana, Shahid, Jugni, Phillauri, Veer de wedding, Manmarziayan, Jai mummy Di, Jawani jaaneman etc. Kuldip Sinha - Kuldip Sinha did his diploma in Cinema from FTII Pune after completing his graduation from University of Pune. Winner of eight national and Five International awards, he served ministry of I&B as a Director General in its Films Division Wing. He was also CEO of Children’s Film Society of India. As a Director he also served 10th and 11th MIFF and 15th Golden Elephant International Children’s Film Festival, Hyderabad. As a prolific writer he has written two novels and five technical books on various aspects of film making. Few of his notable films which won critical acclaim – Gandhi – An emerging reality –(1998)Through a Lens Starkly(1997), No Room for Fear(1996), Toote Pankh(1995) Druzba(1992) Taranath Shenoy(1990), , Services of Trees(1989), Dry Land farming(1985). Presently he is member of Film Censor Board, Mumbai, Film & Television Institute of India, Pune and Satyajit Ray Film & Television institute – Kolkata.

Prashantanu Mahapatra - A post graduate in Cinema from FTII , Prashantanu Mahapatra teaches cinema at various institutes all over Mumbai and Maharashtra. As a freelancer, he has done Cinematography for more than a dozen feature films and fifty short and Documentaries. His award winning films include ‘Breathing without Air’(2004) The Last Monk (2006), Meemansa(2016) and Hello Arsi (2018). Currently he is a faculty member at department of Cinematography, FTII, Pune.
